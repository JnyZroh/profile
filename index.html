Who I am

  • Read a short bio

    These three words seem to define my life:

  • Education
  • Compassion
  • Innovation

I have a Masters in Educational Psychology and career experience seeking to improve learning conditions for students. I decided to learn to code because I was curious if I could create an app to solve a particular problem that college students face as they struggle to manage their time. My curiosity turned into a love for the thrill that coding brings -everytime you fix a bug and you make your feature work.

Le Wagon bootcamp has given me the experience of creating a product from scratch and the confidence to pursue what inspires me. For me, coding fulfills a particular side of me that is not fulfilled in other roles - the side of me that desires to create and innovate. Knowing this, I'm looking forward to my near future where my tech skills can enhance the way I improve the lives of others.
